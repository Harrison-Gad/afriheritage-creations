Symbols We Live By

Gye Nyame

"Except for God" - A symbol of the supremacy of God.

Sankofa

"Go back and get it" - A symbol of the importance of learning from the past.

Adinkrahene

"Chief of the Adinkra symbols" - A symbol of greatness, charisma, and leadership.

Dwennimmen

"Ram's horns" - A symbol of strength yet humility, courage, and wisdom.

Funtunfunefu Denkyemfunefu

"Siamese crocodiles" - A symbol of democracy and unity in diversity.

Nea Onnim

"He who does not know can know from learning" - A symbol of knowledge and life-long education.
